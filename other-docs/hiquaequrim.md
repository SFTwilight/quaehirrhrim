Hı̊Quae'qurĩm
============

teni'iri yiniteshe  
Fĩmir yiyilushi  
a'Rĩf yikisheshi  
an'Te ri'TEI'yoshi.  
an'RAAU hekorinshe  
Rauufĩm enilushi.

I am that I am more,  
That more may live,  
More freely,  
And express their utmost SELVES to me,  
And that together we may understand the ALL,  
That All may live.
